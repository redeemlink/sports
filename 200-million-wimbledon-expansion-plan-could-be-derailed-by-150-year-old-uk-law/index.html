£200 million Wimbledon expansion plan could be derailed by 150-year-old UK law

Source: Google News • 1/17/2026

Wimbledon’s plans to expand its iconic tennis grounds could be blocked by a law that dates back a century and a half, with a High Court ruling now set to decide the project’s fate. The All England Club wants to almost triple the size of its site, adding dozens of new courts and a major show stadium.
