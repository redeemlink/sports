5 Lumberjacks win brackets at Orno Invitational

Source: Google News • 1/25/2026

Jan. 25—ORONO — Out of the six Bemidji High School boys wrestlers who placed in their individual brackets on Saturday at the Orono Invitational, five of them won brackets. The Lumberjacks took third place as a team with 343.5 points, trailing only New Prague (515) and Maple Grove (357). Ozzie Hanks
