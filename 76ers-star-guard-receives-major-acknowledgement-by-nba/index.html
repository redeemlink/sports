76ers star guard receives major acknowledgement by NBA

Source: Google News • 1/19/2026

Philadelphia 76ers star guard Tyrese Maxey has received a major acknowledgement from the NBA community. On Monday, Maxey was officially named as an All-Star starter for the 2025-26 NBA season. It is Maxey’s second time being named an All-Star and his first time as a starter.
