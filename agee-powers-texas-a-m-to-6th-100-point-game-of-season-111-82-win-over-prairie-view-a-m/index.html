Agee powers Texas A&M to 6th 100-point game of season, 111-82 win over Prairie View A&M

Source: Google News • 12/30/2025

Rashaun Agee scored 19 points on 64% shooting and grabbed 13 rebounds, Marcus Hill added 16 points, and Texas A&M rolled to a 111-82 win over Prairie View A&M on Monday night. The Aggies (10-3) notched their sixth game with 100 or more points to set a new single-season program record. Ruben Doming
