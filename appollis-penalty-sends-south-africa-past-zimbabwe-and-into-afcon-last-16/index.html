Appollis penalty sends South Africa past Zimbabwe and into AFCON last-16

Source: Google News • 12/29/2025

Oswin Appollis converted a late penalty to give South Africa a 3-2 Africa Cup of Nations victory over Zimbabwe in Marrakesh on Monday, and qualification for the knockout stage. Appollis then netted from an 82nd-minute penalty to give Bafana the lead for the third time, which they retained.   dl/nr
