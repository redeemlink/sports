Arkansas DL Ian Geffrard plans to enter Transfer Portal

Source: Google News • 12/22/2025

Arkansas Razorback defensive lineman Ian Geffrard plans to enter the Transfer Portal, On3’s Pete Nakos confirmed. He has two season of eligibility remaining. Geffrard played his high school football at Whitefield Academy in Mableton, Georgia. There, he was a three-star recruit in the Class of 2023.
