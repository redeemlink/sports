As Olympics loom, Summer Britcher wins another World Cup gold for USA Luge

USA Luge's Summer Britcher is flying into the Olympics. Britcher got her second women's singles luge win of the season on Sunday, moving her atop the World Cup standings and further cementing her status as a medal contender for the Milan Cortina Olympics next month.
