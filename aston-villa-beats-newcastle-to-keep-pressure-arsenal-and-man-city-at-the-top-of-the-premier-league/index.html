Aston Villa beats Newcastle to keep pressure Arsenal and Man City at the top of the Premier League

Source: JAMES ROBSON • 1/25/2026

Emiliano Buendia and Ollie Watkins scored in each half at St James' Park to move third-place Villa level on points with Manchester City in second on 46 points. Buendia fired Villa ahead with a dipping shot from outside of the area in the 19th minute and Watkins doubled the lead in the 88th.
