Aston Villa signs 17-year-old striker Brian Madjo from Metz in reported $14M deal

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

Aston Villa signed Luxembourg striker Brian Madjo from French club Metz on Monday to strengthen its attacking options. Neither club disclosed the fee for Madjo. Madjo, who completed the transfer on his 17th birthday, was born in north London and has represented England's under-17s.
