Australia captain Alyssa Healy retiring after India series

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

Australia captain Alyssa Healy is retiring following India's upcoming tour, ending a 15-year international career including eight World Cup titles. The 35-year-old Healy has scored more than 7,000 runs across all three international formats with 275 dismissals following her national debut in 2010.
