Bafana Bafana beat Angola 2-1 in winning start to Africa Cup

Source: CIARÁN FAHEY • 12/22/2025

Lyle Foster gave South Africa a winning start to the Africa Cup of Nations by a hard-fought 2-1 over Angola on Monday. The Bafana Bafana ended a six-game winless streak against Angola, which had won three and drawn three of their meetings since a World Cup qualifier in November 2015.
