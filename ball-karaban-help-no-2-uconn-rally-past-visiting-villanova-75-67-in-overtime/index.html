Ball, Karaban help No. 2 UConn rally past visiting Villanova 75-67 in overtime

Source: JIM FULLER • 1/24/2026

Solo Ball had 24 points, including a key 3-pointer in overtime, and Alex Karaban had all of his 17 after halftime as No. 2 UConn beat Villanova 75-67 on Saturday. Silas Demary Jr. and Tarris Reed Jr. each finished with 10 for UConn (19-1, 9-0 Big East), which won its 15th game in a row. Matthew Ho
