Ballygunner and Dingle land All-Ireland titles

Source: Google News • 1/18/2026

It was a Munster double at Croke Park as Ballygunner beat Loughrea 1-20 to 1-14 to win the All-Ireland Senior club Hurling title, as Dingle beat St Brigid's 0-23 to 1-19 to win a classic All-Ireland club Football final.
