Baylor Tennis Tops Illinois in Season Opener

Source: Google News • 1/18/2026

WACO, Texas (FOX 44) — Baylor women’s tennis opened the 2026 season with a 4–3 win over Illinois on Saturday. Kennedy Gibbs led the way with a 6–4, 6–0 singles win and paired with Alian Zack for a 7–5 doubles victory. The 25th-ranked Bears return home Sunday at noon to face Tulane.
