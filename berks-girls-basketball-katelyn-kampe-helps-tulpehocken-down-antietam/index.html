Berks girls basketball: Katelyn Kampe helps Tulpehocken down Antietam

Source: Google News • 1/20/2026

Katelyn Kampe scored 11 points as Tulpehocken defeated Antietam 47-21 in a BCIAA girls basketball game on Monday. Also for the Trojans (4-11, 2-5 Division 4), Alexis Setlock and Molly Goldberg each scored 8 points. Ashanti Dotson scored 11 points for Antietam, which drops to 0-12, 0-7 Division 4. ©
