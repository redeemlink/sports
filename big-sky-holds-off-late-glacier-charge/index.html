Big Sky holds off late Glacier charge

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

Jan. 14—Jack McGowan scored 18 points and Missoula Big Sky held strong down the stretch to defeat Glacier 63-59 in Western AA boys basketball Tuesday at the Wolf Den. Eli Kasberg added 13 points for the Eagles (3-4 overall, 1-2 in Western AA games), who made just one field goal in the final quarter.
