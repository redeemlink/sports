Blackhawks C Connor Bedard steps up his rehab in his return from a shoulder injury

Source: Google News • 1/6/2026

Connor Bedard joined the Chicago Blackhawks for part of Tuesday's practice, stepping up his rehab in his return from a right shoulder injury. Bedard got hurt on a draw with 0.8 seconds left in a 3-2 loss at St. Louis on Dec. 12. “I feel really good,” Bedard said.
