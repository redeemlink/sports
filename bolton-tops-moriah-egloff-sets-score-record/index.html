Bolton tops Moriah, Egloff sets score record

Source: Google News • 1/13/2026

MORIAH — At halftime Bolton held a 16 point lead over Moriah. The Eagles would need all of that to hold on and win 48-42 Monday in a non-conference matchup. It was a memorable night for Bolton senior Jadynn Egloff. During the game, she set a new school record in girls’ basketball for points scored.
