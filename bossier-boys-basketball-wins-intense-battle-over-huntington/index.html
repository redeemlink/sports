Bossier boys basketball wins intense battle over Huntington

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

BOSSIER, La (KTAL/KMSS) — The intense rivalry between Bossier and Huntington continued Tuesday night as the two schools met on the hardwood. After a physical four quarters of basketball, the Bearkats came out on top 78-58. Bossier was led by D.T. Thomas, who finished with a team high 21 points.
