Brahim Díaz shoots Morocco into the Africa Cup of Nations quarterfinals with 1-0 win over Tanzania

Source: CIARÁN FAHEY • 1/4/2026

Brahim Díaz kept Morocco on course for the Africa Cup of Nations title by shooting the team into the quarterfinals with a 1-0 win over Tanzania on Sunday. Díaz, who has quickly emerged as the tournament host's star player, broke the deadlock in the 64th minute.
