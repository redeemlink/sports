Brandon Valley wins Floyd Farrand Tournament

Source: Google News • 12/31/2025

SIOUX FALLS, S.D. (KELO) — The Brandon Valley boys wrestling team won the 2025 Floyd Farrand Invitational with a strong showing on Tuesday. The Lynx posted 260 teams points to claim the crown. Watertown finished second at 224 points. The Canton girls won the Floyd Farrand Invitational on Monday.
