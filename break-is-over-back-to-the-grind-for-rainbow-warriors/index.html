Break is over, back to the grind for Rainbow Warriors

Source: Google News • 1/1/2026

The Hawaii basketball team’s farewell season continues with today’s matchup of islanders and Highlanders in Riverside, Calif. After an 18-day hiatus, the Rainbow Warriors resume league play in their final Big West season. On July 1, the’Bows leave the Big West after 13 seasons for the Mountain West.
