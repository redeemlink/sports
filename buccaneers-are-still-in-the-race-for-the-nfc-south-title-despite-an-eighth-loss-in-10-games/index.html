Buccaneers are still in the race for the NFC South title despite an eighth loss in 10 games

Source: ROB MAADDI • 12/29/2025

Wide receiver Mike Evans said the team played like it was ready to go home. The Tampa Bay Buccaneers have collapsed in the second half but still remain alive in the playoff race despite a 20-17 loss at Miami on Sunday. The Buccaneers started the franchise’s 50th season 3-0 and were 6-2 at the bye.
