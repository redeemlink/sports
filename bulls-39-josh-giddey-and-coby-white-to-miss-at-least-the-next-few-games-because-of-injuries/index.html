Bulls&#39; Josh Giddey and Coby White to miss at least the next few games because of injuries

Source: Google News • 12/31/2025

The Chicago Bulls will have to get by without their top two scorers for at least the next few games, with Josh Giddey sidelined because of a strained left hamstring and Coby White out with right calf tightness. The team said Wednesday that Giddey will be reevaluated in two weeks and White in one.
