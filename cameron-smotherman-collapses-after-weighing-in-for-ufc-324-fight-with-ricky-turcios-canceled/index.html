Cameron Smotherman collapses after weighing in for UFC 324, fight with Ricky Turcios canceled

Source: W.G. RAMIREZ • 1/23/2026

Bantamweight fighter Cameron Smotherman collapsed Friday afternoon immediately after weighing in and his bout with Ricky Turcios at UFC 324 was canceled. Smotherman made his way to the stage seemingly without any complications. Smotherman hit his head and chin on the stage.
