Celebrini extends point streak to 12 games as Sharks beat Kings 4-3 in overtime

Source: DAN GREENSPAN • 1/8/2026

William Eklund scored 3:08 into overtime, Macklin Celebrini had the tying goal and two assists to extend his point streak to 12 games, and the San Jose Sharks defeated the Los Angeles Kings 4-3 on Wednesday night. Celebrini evened the score at 3 with 1:07 remaining in regulation.
