Celtics make 20 3-pointers in 140-122 win over Pacers

Source: The Associated Press • 12/27/2025

Jaylen Brown scored 30 points and the Boston Celtics made 20 3-pointers in a 140-122 victory over the Indiana Pacers on Friday night. Payton Pritchard added 29 points, making three 3s, and grabbed nine rebounds. The Celtics were 20 of 39 from beyond the arc compared to the Pacers’ 18 of 44.
