College Football TV Ratings: ESPN, ABC draw strong viewership for CFP 1st round

Source: Google News • 12/23/2025

The second installment of the 12-team College Football Playoff brought more strong TV ratings for ESPN and ABC. All told, the first round averaged 9.9 million viewers across ESPN, ABC, TNT and truTV.
