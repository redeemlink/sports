Columbus Blue Jackets fire coach Dean Evason, hire Rick Bowness to replace him

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

The Columbus Blue Jackets fired coach Dean Evason and named Rick Bowness his replacement on Monday. Evason is the first NHL coach fired this season. General manager Don Waddell announced the abrupt change with his team sitting in last place in the Eastern Conference 45 games in.
