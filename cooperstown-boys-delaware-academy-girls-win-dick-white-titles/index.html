Cooperstown boys, Delaware Academy girls win Dick White titles

Source: Google News • 12/30/2025

Two defending section champions won titles Tuesday, Dec. 30, at Cooperstown’s Dick White Holiday Tournament at Red Bursey Gymnasium.
