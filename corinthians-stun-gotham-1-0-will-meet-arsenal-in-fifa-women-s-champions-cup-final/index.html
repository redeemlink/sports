Corinthians stun Gotham 1-0, will meet Arsenal in FIFA Women’s Champions Cup final

Source: Google News • 1/28/2026

Gabi Zanotti’s late goal against NJ/NY Gotham secured a momentous 1-0 win for SC Corinthians at the Gtech Community Stadium in London on Wednesday. The Brazilian side advances to the inaugural FIFA Women’s Champions Cup final, where they will meet Arsenal on Sunday at 1 p.m. ET at Emirates Stadium.
