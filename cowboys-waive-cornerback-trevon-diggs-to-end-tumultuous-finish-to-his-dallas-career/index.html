Cowboys waive cornerback Trevon Diggs to end tumultuous finish to his Dallas career

Source: SCHUYLER DIXON • 12/30/2025

The Cowboys waived cornerback Trevon Diggs on Tuesday, ending a tumultuous final season a game early in a Dallas career that never lived up to the promise of a 2021 All-Pro performance that included 11 interceptions. The move came five days before Dallas' season finale Sunday at the New York Giants
