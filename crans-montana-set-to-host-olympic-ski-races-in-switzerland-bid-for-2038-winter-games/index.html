Crans-Montana set to host Olympic ski races in Switzerland bid for 2038 Winter Games

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

Crans-Montana is set to host Alpine ski races at the 2038 Olympics as organizers of the Switzerland bid detailed their plan of venues Monday. The Swiss Olympic body is the only candidate in talks with the International Olympic Committee to be awarded the Winter Games taking place in 12 years’ time.
