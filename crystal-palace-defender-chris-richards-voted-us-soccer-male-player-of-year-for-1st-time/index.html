Crystal Palace defender Chris Richards voted US Soccer Male Player of Year for 1st time

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

Defender Chris Richards was voted the U.S. Soccer Federation Male Player of the Year for the first time on Wednesday. Richards played in 12 U.S. matches in 2025, starting 11 of them. The 25-year-old from Birmingham, Alabama, scored twice in the CONCACAF Gold Cup.
