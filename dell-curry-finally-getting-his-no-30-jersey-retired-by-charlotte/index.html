Dell Curry finally getting his No. 30 jersey retired by Charlotte

Source: TIM REYNOLDS • 1/14/2026

Dell Curry had stopped wondering if the Charlotte Hornets were going to retire his number. The Hornets announced Wednesday that Curry — the father of Stephen and Seth Curry — will get his No. 30 retired in a ceremony on March 23 when the team plays host to the Orlando Magic.
