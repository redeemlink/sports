Doncic and James lead Lakers&#39; rally to beat shorthanded Grizzlies 128-121

Source: BETH HARRIS • 1/3/2026

Luka Doncic scored 34 points — half on free throws — and LeBron James added 31 as the Los Angeles Lakers rallied to beat the short-handed Memphis Grizzlies 128-121 on Friday night to open a two-game set. The teams will meet again Sunday in Los Angeles. James had nine rebounds and six assists.
