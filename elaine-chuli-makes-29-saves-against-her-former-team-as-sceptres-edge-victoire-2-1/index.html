Elaine Chuli makes 29 saves against her former team as Sceptres edge Victoire 2-1

Source: Google News • 12/27/2025

Elaine Chuli made 29 saves as the Toronto Sceptres defeated the Montreal Victoire 2-1 on Saturday at the Bell Centre. It was Chuli’s first start against Montreal after spending the first two years in the league as the Victoire’s backup goaltender. Jesse Compher and Daryl Watts scored for Toronto.
