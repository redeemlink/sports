Elina Svitolina beats Wang Xinyu to win her 19th WTA Tour title in Auckland

Source: Google News • 1/11/2026

Elina Svitolina will head to the Australian Open after winning her 19th WTA Tour title Sunday at the ASB Classic in Auckland, New Zealand. Top seed Svitolina beat seventh-seeded Wang Xinyu of China 6-3, 7-6 (8). It was the 13th-ranked Svitolina's 19th title from 24 finals appearances.
