Ex-Stuttgart and Hoffenheim boss Matarazzo takes over Sociedad

Source: Google News • 12/21/2025

Former VfB Stuttgart and Hoffenheim coach Pellegrino Matarazzo is the new boss of Real Sociedad. The 48-year-old American takes over at the relegation-threatened La Liga club on a deal until the end of next season, the San Sebastian side announced late on Saturday.
