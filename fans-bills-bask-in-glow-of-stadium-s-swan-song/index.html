Fans, Bills bask in glow of stadium's swan song

Source: www.espn.com - TOP • 1/5/2026

In what is likely to be the final game at Highmark Stadium, the Buffalo Bills on Sunday handled the New York Jets, 35-8, much to the delight of their loyal fans, who often braved wet and frigid elements in the Orchard Park, New York facility for 53 years.
