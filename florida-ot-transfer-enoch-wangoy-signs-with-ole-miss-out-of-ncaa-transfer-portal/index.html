Florida OT transfer Enoch Wangoy signs with Ole Miss out of NCAA Transfer Portal

Source: Google News • 1/18/2026

Florida Florida redshirt freshman offensive tackle Enoch Wangoy has signed to transfer to Ole Miss out of the NCAA Transfer Portal, according to On3’s Pete Nakos. Wangoy will have three more years of eligibility remaining in Oxford. This report will be updated.
