Former Appalachian State DB DJ Burks commits to Tennessee

Source: Google News • 1/17/2026

Former Appalachian State defensive back DJ Burks committed to Tennessee out of the NCAA transfer portal, On3 has learned. He spent four years with the program before transferring. Last season, Burks had 73 tackles, one tackle for loss, two interceptions, five pass deflections and two forced fumbles.
