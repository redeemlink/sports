Former Celtics Guard Suspended Following Luka Doncic Incident

Source: Google News • 1/11/2026

Sacramento Kings guard Dennis Schroder was suspended three games on Saturday, two weeks following a postgame incident with Los Angeles Lakers star Luka Doncic. The former Boston Celtics guard was handed the punishment after attempting to strike Doncic after the Kings’ loss to the Lakers on Dec. 28.
