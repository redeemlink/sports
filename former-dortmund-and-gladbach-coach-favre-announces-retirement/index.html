Former Dortmund and Gladbach coach Favre announces retirement

Source: Google News • 12/21/2025

Former Bundesliga coach Lucien Favre has announced his retirement at the age of 68. The former Hertha Berlin, Borussia Mönchengladbach and Borussia Dortmund boss has been out of work for nearly three years since leaving Nice and has now called it a day.
