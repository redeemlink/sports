Former Germany defender Mertesacker to quit as Arsenal academy boss

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

Germany 2014 World Cup winner Per Mertesacker will leave his role as Arsenal's head of youth development at the end of the season after eight years, the Premier League leaders said on Monday. The 41-year-old former Hanover, Werder Bremen and Arsenal player said he wanted to take on a new challenge.
