Frankfurt sign French striker Kalimuendo on loan from Nottingham

Source: Google News • 1/8/2026

Eintracht Frankfurt have further bolstered their attack by signing French centre forward Arnaud Kalimuendo from Nottingham Forest. Frankfurt said on Wednesday night that Kalimuendo arrives from England on loan for the rest of the season, with an option to buy.
