Frankfurt snatch stoppage time 3-3 in Bremen

Source: Google News • 1/16/2026

Eintracht Frankfurt provided plenty of Bundesliga drama once again when they snatched a stoppage time 3-3 draw at Werder Bremen on Friday night. Ansgar Knauff scored in the fourth minute of time added on after a cross from Ayoube Amaimouni-Echghouyab.
