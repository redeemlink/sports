French coach Gasset dies at age 72

Source: Google News • 12/26/2025

Former French football player and coach Jean-Louis Gasset has died at age 72, his former club Montpellier and French media said on Friday. Gasset played for 10 years at Montpellier and coached the club from his home town on three occasions, the last term until April 2025.
