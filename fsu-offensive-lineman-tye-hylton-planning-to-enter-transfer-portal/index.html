FSU offensive lineman Tye Hylton planning to enter transfer portal

Source: Google News • 12/30/2025

Florida State offensive lineman Tye Hylton will enter the transfer portal next month, he shared with On3’s Hayes Fawcett. The former three-star recruit didn’t appear on the field for the Seminoles over the last two seasons. With his departure, just seven players from FSU’s 2023 signing class remain.
