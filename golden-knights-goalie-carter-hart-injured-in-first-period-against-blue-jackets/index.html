Golden Knights goalie Carter Hart injured in first period against Blue Jackets

Source: MARK ANDERSON • 1/9/2026

Vegas Golden Knights goalie Carter Hart appeared to injure his left leg in the first period Thursday night against the Columbus Blue Jackets. Columbus had possession of the puck, forcing an injured Hart to stay in the crease while his teammates tried to defend. Akira Schmid replaced Hart.
