Harbaugh out as Ravens coach after playoff miss

Source: Jamison Hensley • 1/6/2026

The Ravens fired John Harbaugh on Tuesday, less than 48 hours after losing to the Steelers and failing to reach the playoffs. Harbaugh led Baltimore for 18 seasons, finishing with a 193-124 record and a Super Bowl title in the 2012 season.
