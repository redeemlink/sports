Head finally out for 163 as Bethell gets first Ashes wicket

Source: BBC Sport • 1/6/2026

England finally bring an end to Travis Head's innings as he's trapped lbw for 163 by Jacob Bethell as he picks up his first Ashes wicket, to leave Australia 288-4 in their first innings, trailing England by 96 runs on day three of the fifth Ashes Test in Sydney.
