Hoosiers Stay Perfect at Home, Beat Washington 90-10 in Big Ten Re-Start

Source: Google News • 1/5/2026

Indiana had 13 days between games before getting back to work on Sunday night against Washington. They didn't look rusty at all, scoring 35 points in the first 10 minutes and cruising to a 90-80 victory to get the Big Ten season restarted. Indiana is 10-0 at home this year.
