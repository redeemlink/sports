Igor Thiago sets record for most goals by a Brazilian in a Premier League season

Source: Google News • 1/7/2026

There have been plenty of top Brazilian players in the Premier League. None have scored more in a single campaign than Igor Thiago. The 24-year-old striker made it 16 goals for the season in the league with a double for Brentford in its 3-0 win over Sunderland on Wednesday.
