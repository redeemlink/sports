In his 13th year, Baltimore NT John Jenkins finally receives an in-season contract extension

Source: NOAH TRISTER • 1/2/2026

At age 36, John Jenkins finally received a contract extension in the NFL. The Ravens announced Friday they were keeping the veteran nose tackle. The 6-foot-3, 360-pound Jenkins is in his 13th pro season, but receiving an extension before the end of the season is a novelty for him.
