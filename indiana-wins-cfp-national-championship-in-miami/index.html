Indiana wins CFP National Championship in Miami

Source: Google News • 1/20/2026

HENDERSON, Ky. (WEHT) — The Indiana Hoosiers have won the CFP National Championship. The Hoosiers took down the Miami Hurricanes, 27-21 on Monday. This is the first time the Hoosiers have won the championship. The Hoosiers are also undefeated.
