Jamestown High School athletics events postponed

Source: Google News • 1/23/2026

Jan. 22—JAMESTOWN — Due to extreme cold, several Jamestown High School athletics events scheduled for Jan. 22 have been postponed. The Jamestown High School girls and boys wrestling duals against Minot United have been postponed as has the Jamestown High School girls hockey game against Grand Forks.
