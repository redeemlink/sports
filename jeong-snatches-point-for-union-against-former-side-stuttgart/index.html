Jeong snatches point for Union against former side Stuttgart

Source: Google News • 1/18/2026

VfB Stuttgart nudged up into the Bundesliga top four but their former player Jeong Woo Yeong grabbed a point for visitors Union Berlin in a 1-1 draw on Sunday. The South Korean substitute refused to celebrate after levelling on 83 minutes after Chris Führich had given the hosts the lead.
