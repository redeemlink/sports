Jeong snatches Union late draw at Stuttgart

Source: Google News • 1/18/2026

Union Berlin's Woo-yeong Jeong scored with seven minutes remaining to snatch his side a point in a 1-1 draw at Stuttgart in the Bundesliga on Sunday. The miss came back to haunt the hosts almost immediately when former Stuttgart forward Jeong sliced his shot into the top corner in the 83rd minute.
