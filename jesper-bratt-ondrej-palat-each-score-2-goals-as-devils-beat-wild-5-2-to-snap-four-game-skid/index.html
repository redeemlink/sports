Jesper Bratt, Ondrej Palat each score 2 goals as Devils beat Wild 5-2 to snap four-game skid

Source: Google News • 1/13/2026

Jesper Bratt and Ondrej Palat each scored twice and the New Jersey Devils beat the Minnesota Wild 5-2 on Monday night to snap a four-game skid. Dawson Mercer had a goal and an assist for New Jersey, and Nico Hischier and Dougie Hamilton each had two assists. Jacob Markstrom finished with 20 saves.
