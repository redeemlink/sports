Jets defense continues to spiral in loss to Saints

Source: Google News • 12/21/2025

The December blues continue for the New York Jets. The New Orleans Saints (5-10), behind five field goals by Charlie Smyth, defeated the Jets (3-12) 29-6 at Caesars Superdome. New Orleans won its third consecutive game while the Jets lost their third in a row and fourth out of their last five games.
