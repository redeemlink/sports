Juventus beats Roma 2-1 to move within a point of top four in Serie A

Source: DANIELLA MATAR • 12/20/2025

Roma missed the chance to go to the top of Serie A after losing at Juventus 2-1 on Saturday. Francisco Conceição and Loïs Openda scored for Juventus before Tommaso Baldanzi set up a frantic finale. Juventus moved to within one point of fourth-placed Roma.
