Kalimuendo joins Frankfurt from Forest

Source: Google News • 1/7/2026

Striker Arnaud Kalimuendo has joined Eintracht Frankfurt on an initial loan deal. The Bundesliga club have an option to make the move permanent for about £23m. Forest are keen on Wolves forward Jorgen Strand Larsen as they look for more firepower in January.
