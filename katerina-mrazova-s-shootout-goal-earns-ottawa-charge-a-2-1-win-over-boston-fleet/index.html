Katerina Mrazova’s shootout goal earns Ottawa Charge a 2-1 win over Boston Fleet

Source: Google News • 1/11/2026

Katerina Mrazova’s shootout goal earned the Ottawa Charge a 2-1 win over the Boston Fleet at Scotiabank Centre on Sunday. Fanuza Kadirova and Brianne Jenner also scored in the shootout for Ottawa. Jenner also scored for in regulation and Sanni Ahola made 31 saves for the Charge.
