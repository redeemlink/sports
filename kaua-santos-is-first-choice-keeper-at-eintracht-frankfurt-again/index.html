Kaua Santos is first choice keeper at Eintracht Frankfurt again

Source: Google News • 1/8/2026

Kaua Santos has been reinstated as Eintracht Frankfurt first choice goalkeeper going into the second part of the Bundesliga season, coach Dino Toppmöller said on Thursday. Santos will be between the posts on Friday against Borussia Dortmund.
