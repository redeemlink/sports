Klæbo and Skistad start Tour de Ski with sprint wins

Source: Google News • 12/28/2025

Norwegians Johannes Høsflot Klæbo and Kristine Stavås Skistad kicked off this season's Tour de Ski with a cross country sprint win in Toblach, Italy on Sunday. For sprint king Klæbo it was business as usual amid a dominant World Cup season.
