Lauren Betts named Big Ten Player of the Week for fifth-time in her career

A week of dominance against tough Big Ten opponents turns into UCLA senior center Lauren Betts' fifth-career Big Ten Player of the Week selection. It's the second time this season that Betts was given the honor, the first being the week of Dec. 8.
