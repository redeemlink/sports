Leeds enquire about Wolves striker Strand Larsen

Source: Google News • 1/16/2026

Leeds have enquired about buying Wolves striker Jorgen Strand Larsen. The Elland Road side have been quoted £40m for the Norway international but are yet to make a firm bid. Leeds, 16th in the Premier League, have already signed Brighton attacker Facundo Buonanotte on loan this week.
