Left fielder Tyler Soderstrom reaches $86M, 7-year agreement with Athletics, AP source says

Source: JANIE McCAULEY • 12/26/2025

Left fielder Tyler Soderstrom and the Athletics have agreed to an $86 million, seven-year contract, according to a person with knowledge of the negotiations. The team is entering the second of three planned seasons at a Triple-A ballpark in West Sacramento.
