Lens move four points clear of PSG at top of Ligue 1

Source: Google News • 1/2/2026

Lens notched their seventh successive win to beat Toulouse 3-0 on Friday and move four points clear of Paris Saint-Germain at the top of Ligue 1. Lens have held top spot since November 30 and will stay there for at least another fortnight as next weekend is given over to the French Cup.
