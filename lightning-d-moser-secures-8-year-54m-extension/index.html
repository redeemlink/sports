Lightning D Moser secures 8-year, $54M extension

Source: Ryan Clark • 12/27/2025

On the day his club returned from its holiday break to meet the Florida Panthers, defenseman J.J. Moser, who has three goals and 12 points, signed a long-term contract with the Tampa Bay Lightning, securing an eight-year deal worth $6.75 million annually.
