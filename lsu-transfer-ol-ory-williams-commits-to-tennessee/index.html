LSU transfer OL Ory Williams commits to Tennessee

Source: Google News • 1/6/2026

Former LSU offensive lineman Ory Williams has committed to Tennessee, via the NCAA Transfer Portal. Williams spent two seasons at LSU. Williams made 12 appearances and two starts in the 2025 campaign for the Tigers. As a true freshman, Williams played in just one game before ultimately redshirting.
