Maryland prioritizes recruiting DMV high school stars: ‘You can stay home’

Source: Google News • 12/23/2025

Malik Washington was not the first blue-chip prospect to agree to play at Maryland, and he will not be the last. But he is part of a growing group of high school sports stars in the region surrounding Washington, D.C. — known as the DMV — who have elected to stay home rather than compete elsewhere.
