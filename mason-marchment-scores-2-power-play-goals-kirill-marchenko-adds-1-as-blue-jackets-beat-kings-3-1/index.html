Mason Marchment scores 2 power-play goals, Kirill Marchenko adds 1 as Blue Jackets beat Kings 3-1

Source: DAN GREENSPAN • 12/23/2025

Mason Marchment scored two power-play goals, Kirill Marchenko had one, and the Columbus Blue Jackets beat the Los Angeles Kings 3-1 on Monday night. Jet Greaves made 23 saves and Damon Severson had two assists as Columbus snapped a four-game road losing streak.
