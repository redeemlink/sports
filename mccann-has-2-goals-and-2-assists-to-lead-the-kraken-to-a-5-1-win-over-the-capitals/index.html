McCann has 2 goals and 2 assists to lead the Kraken to a 5-1 win over the Capitals

Source: MARK MOSCHETTI • 1/28/2026

Jared McCann had two goals and two assists as the Seattle Kraken beat the Washington Capitals 5-1 on Tuesday night. Jordan Eberle and Matty Beniers each added a goal and an assist. Ryker Evans also scored for Seattle, and Philipp Grubauer made 19 saves.
