McMichael and Ovechkin score as the Capitals beat the depleted Blackhawks 5-1

Source: JAY COHEN • 1/10/2026

Connor McMichael had a goal and an assist, and the Washington Capitals snapped Chicago's four-game win streak with a 5-1 over the depleted Blackhawks on Friday night. Alex Ovechkin, Anthony Beauvillier, Ethen Frank and Justin Sourdif also scored for Washington, which had lost three of four.
