Mia Pauldo scores career-high 26, No. 20 Lady Vols beat Mississippi State 90-80

Source: Google News • 1/9/2026

Freshman Mia Pauldo scored a season-high 26 points, Janiah Barker added 18, and No. 20 Tennessee beat Mississippi State 90-80 on Thursday night. Tennessee (11-3, 3-0 SEC) has won four straight and six of seven. The Lady Vols are 42-8 in the series with Mississippi State that began in 1986.
