MLB hands German outfielder Kepler 80-game doping ban

Source: Google News • 1/9/2026

German free agent outfielder Max Kepler was issued an 80-game doping ban by Major League Baseball on Friday after testing positive for the performance-enhancing substance Epitrenbolone.After working his way through the minor-league development system, Kepler made his MLB debut for the Twins in 2015.
