Monmouth transfer DL Lamont Lester Jr. commits to Colorado

Source: Google News • 1/4/2026

Monmouth transfer redshirt freshman DL Lamont Lester Jr. has committed to Colorado, On3’s Pete Nakos reports. Lester Jr. was named an FCS Freshman All-American this past season. Lester Jr. is the second defensive transfer to commit to Colorado Saturday night, joining former Charlotte DL Yamil Talib.
