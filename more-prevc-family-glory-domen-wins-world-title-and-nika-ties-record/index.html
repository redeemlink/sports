More Prevc family glory: Domen wins world title and Nika ties record

Source: Google News • 1/24/2026

Domen Prevc destroyed the opposition to claim the ski flying world title in Germany on a Saturday when his sister, Nika Prevc, tied a women's ski jumping World Cup record in Japan. Domen Prevc emulated his older brother, Peter Prevc, for the second time this season with the flying title.
