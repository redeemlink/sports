Morgan helps Kentucky rally from 13-point deficit to beat Oklahoma 63-57

Source: KEITH TAYLOR • 1/11/2026

Tonie Morgan scored 21 points to lead No. 6 Kentucky to a 63-57 win over fifth-ranked Oklahoma on Sunday. Kentucky opened conference play with an 80-78 win over 12th-ranked LSU on Jan. 1 in Baton Rouge. The Tigers were ranked No. 5 at the time.
