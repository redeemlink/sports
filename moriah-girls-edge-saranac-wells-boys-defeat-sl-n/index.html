Source: Google News • 1/20/2026

PORT HENRY — Lexi Cheney led a balanced attack with 10 points, and Moriah edged out a 37-36 win over Saranac on Friday night in a low-scoring Champlain Valley Athletic Conference girls’ basketball contest.
