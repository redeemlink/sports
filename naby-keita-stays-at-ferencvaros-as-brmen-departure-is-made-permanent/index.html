Naby Keita stays at Ferencvaros as Brmen departure is made permanent

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

Former Liverpool midfielder Naby Keita has joined Hungarian champions Ferencvaros on a permanent deal from Werder Bremen, both clubs said on Wednesday. Keita, 30, was a surprise Bremen signing in 2023 but he featured in only five games and went to Ferencvaros on a year-long loan in January 2025.
