Nate Ament helps Tennessee top No. 17 Alabama, spoiling debut of Charles Bediako

Source: KENNINGTON SMITH III • 1/25/2026

Tennessee freshman Nate Ament scored a career-high 29 points, Ja'K Gillespie added 23 and the Volunteers upset No. 17 Alabama 79-73 on Saturday night. The loss spolied the debut of Charles Bediako for Alabama. Bediako received a loud ovation when he checked into the game nearly 4 minutes in.
