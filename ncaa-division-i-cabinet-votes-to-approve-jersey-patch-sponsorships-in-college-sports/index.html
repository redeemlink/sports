NCAA Division I Cabinet votes to approve jersey patch sponsorships in college sports

Source: Google News • 1/23/2026

In an anticipated move, the NCAA Division I Cabinet voted to approve jersey patch sponsorships in college sports. The legislation is set to take effect Aug. 1, just in time for the 2026 college football season. The move will add another revenue stream for schools with millions of dollars in sponsors
