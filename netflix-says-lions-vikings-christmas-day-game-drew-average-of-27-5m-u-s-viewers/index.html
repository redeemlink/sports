Netflix says Lions-Vikings Christmas day game drew average of 27.5M U.S. viewers

Source: Google News • 1/1/2026

Netflix (NFLX) said its second NFL Christmas Gameday contest between the Detroit Lions and Minnesota Vikings became the most-streamed NFL game in U.S. history with an average of 27.5M viewers in the U.S., with US viewership peaking at over 30M viewers, according to Nielsen Big Data + Panel.
