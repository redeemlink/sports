No. 1 Arizona stays undefeated as Koa Peat scores 20 points in an 86-73 win at TCU

Source: SCHUYLER DIXON • 1/10/2026

Freshman Koa Peat had 20 points and seven rebounds, Anthony Dell'Orso and Jaden Bradley scored 17 points apiece and top-ranked Arizona beat TCU 86-73 on Saturday. The victory came a few hours after No. 2 Michigan's 91-88 loss to Wisconsin reduced the number of undefeated teams to five. Tanner Tool
