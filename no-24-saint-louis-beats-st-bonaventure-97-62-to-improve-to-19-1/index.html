No. 24 Saint Louis beats St. Bonaventure 97-62 to improve to 19-1

Source: MARK LUDWICZAK • 1/24/2026

Ishan Sharma scored 29 points and No. 24 Saint Louis routed St. Bonaventure 97-62 on Friday. The Billikens (19-1, 7-0 Atlantic 10) tied their best start 20-game start in the program’s 110-year history, joining the 1993-94 team. Trey Green had 15 points for Saint Louis and Amari McCottry added 12.
