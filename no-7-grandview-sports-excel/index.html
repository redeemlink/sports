No. 7: Grandview sports excel

Source: Google News • 1/3/2026

The Grandview Lady Zebras secured the softball team’s first state championship in school history. The team compiled a 35-3 record in 2025 en route to capturing the UIL Class 2A Division I State Championship on May 29. This year marked the team’s third consecutive appearance in the state tournament.
