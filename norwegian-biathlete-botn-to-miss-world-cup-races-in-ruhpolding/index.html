Norwegian biathlete Botn to miss World Cup races in Ruhpolding

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

Norwegian biathlete Johan-Olav Botn will miss the men's World Cup races in Ruhpolding, Germany this week, the Norwegian federation told broadcasters NRK ahead of the start of the event on Wednesday. The 26-year-old had already missed the races in Germany's Oberhof the week before due health reasons
