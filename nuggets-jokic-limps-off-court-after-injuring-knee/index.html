Nuggets' Jokic limps off court after injuring knee

Source: www.espn.com - TOP • 12/30/2025

Nuggets star Nikola Jokic limped off the court at halftime of Denver's game in Miami on Monday night after injuring his left knee with about three seconds left before the intermission. He will have an MRI on Tuesday.
