Oklahoma offensive lineman Jacob Sexton plans to enter NCAA Transfer Portal

Source: Google News • 12/23/2025

Oklahoma offensive lineman Jacob Sexton plans to enter the NCAA Transfer Portal, per On3’s Pete Nakos. He’s started at multiple positions along the offensive line for the Sooners over his time in Norma. Sexton played high school football at Deer Creek (Edmond, OK), where he was a four-star prospect.
