Okot, Edwards lead sluggish No. 3 South Carolina to 20th straight win over Georgia 65-43

Source: PETE IACOBELLI • 1/11/2026

Madina Okot had 14 points and 10 rebounds while Joyce Edwards scored 14 as No. 3 South Carolina won its 20th straight over Georgia 65-43 on Sunday. It was was sluggish offense performance for the Gamecocks (17-1, 4-0 Southeastern), who overcame 38% shooting — they came in second in the SEC at 52.6%
