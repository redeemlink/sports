Ole Miss defeats Georgia in Sugar Bowl to advance in College Football Playoffs

Source: Google News • 1/2/2026

NEW ORLEANS, La. (WJTV) – No. 6 Ole Miss defeated No. 3 Georgia in the second round of the College Football Playoffs. The Rebels beat the Bulldogs 39 to 34. Ole Miss will face No. 10 Miami in the Fiesta Bowl on January 8, 2026, at 6:30 p.m. CT at State Farm Stadium in Arizona.
