On This Day, Dec. 30: Becky Hammon is first woman to coach NBA regular season game

Source: Google News • 12/30/2025

On Dec. 30, 2020, Becky Hammon of the San Antonio Spurs made history as the first woman to coach an NBA regular season game when she filled in for Greg Popovich after he was ejected during a game against the Los Angeles Lakers.
