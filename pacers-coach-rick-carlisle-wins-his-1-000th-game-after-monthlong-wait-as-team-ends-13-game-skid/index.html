Pacers coach Rick Carlisle wins his 1,000th game after monthlong wait as team ends 13-game skid

Source: MICHAEL MAROT • 1/9/2026

Indiana Pacers coach Rick Carlisle finally got a chance to celebrate Thursday night. Carlisle became the 11th coach to reach the milestone when the Pacers won 114-112 at Charlotte. All it took was for the Pacers to score final five points in the final minute against the Hornets.
