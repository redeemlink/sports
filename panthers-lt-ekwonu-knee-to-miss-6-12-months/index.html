Panthers LT Ekwonu (knee) to miss 6-12 months

Source: David Newton • 1/11/2026

Panthers starting left tackle Ickey Ekwonu suffered a ruptured patellar tendon in his right knee in Saturday's playoff loss. With a recovery time of 6-12 months, coach Dave Canales said the injury will force the team to alter its offseason plans.
