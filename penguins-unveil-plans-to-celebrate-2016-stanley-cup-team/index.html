Penguins unveil plans to celebrate 2016 Stanley Cup team

Source: Google News • 1/23/2026

The Pittsburgh Penguins announced plans to celebrate the team’s 2016 Stanley Cup championship later this month. When the Penguins host the New York Rangers on January 31, the team says it will be having a reunion to mark the 10-year anniversary of the organization’s fourth Stanley Cup championship.
