Pressly, 37, world champion with Astros, retires

Source: www.espn.com - TOP • 1/18/2026

Two-time All-Star reliever Ryan Pressly, who collected 117 saves and helped the Houston Astros win the 2022 World Series, retired after a 13-year career,  the 37-year-old pitcher announced Saturday.
