Pressure all on Aberdeen in cup, says Imrie

Source: Google News • 1/17/2026

Dougie Imrie hopes his Raith Rovers side can benefit from the pressure on Aberdeen in Sunday's Scottish Cup tie, which is live on BBC One Scotland and iPlayer. "The pressure is all on Aberdeen," said Imrie. Imrie is also hopeful striker Lewis Vaughan could be fit in time for the trip to Pittodrie.
