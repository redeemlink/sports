QB Mensah can enter portal, can't sign until ruling

Source: Mark Schlabach and Pete Thamel • 1/21/2026

Duke QB Darian Mensah is permitted to enter the transfer portal, but can't enroll at another school or play football for another college team before a North Carolina judge rules on Duke's request for an injunction that would prevent him from leaving.
