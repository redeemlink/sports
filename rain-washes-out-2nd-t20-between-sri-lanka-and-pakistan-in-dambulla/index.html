Rain washes out 2nd T20 between Sri Lanka and Pakistan in Dambulla

Source: Google News • 1/9/2026

Rain washed out the second Twenty20 between Sri Lanka and Pakistan on Friday. Persistent rain in Dambulla forced the match officials to call off the game at 8:37 p.m. local time. Pakistan leads the three-match series 1-0 after beating Sri Lanka by six wickets on Wednesday.
