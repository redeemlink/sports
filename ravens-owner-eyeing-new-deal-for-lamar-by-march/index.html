Ravens owner eyeing new deal for Lamar by March

Source: www.espn.com - TOP • 1/14/2026

Baltimore Ravens owner Steve Bisciotti told reporters Tuesday that he's told star quarterback Lamar Jackson he wants a new deal done by the start of free agency in March, one that would significantly lower the two-time NFL MVP's $74.5 million cap number.
