Recalled Ndiaye takes Senegal past 10-man Mali into AFCON semis

Source: Google News • 1/9/2026

Recalled Iliman Ndiaye scored in the first half to give Senegal a 1-0 victory over 10-man Mali in Tangiers on Friday in the first Africa Cup of Nations quarter-final."It was a difficult match for us because we had to play (half) of it with 10 men," said Mali coach Tom Saintfiet.
