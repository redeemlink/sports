Red Wings outlast Capitals 3-2 in OT to sweep home-and-home series

Source: DAVE HOGG • 12/21/2025

Moritz Seider scored at 4:36 of overtime and the Detroit Red Wings beat the Washington Capitals 3-2 on Sunday to sweep a home-and-home series. Detroit won 5-2 in Washington on Saturday. The Red Wings have won seven of their last nine.
