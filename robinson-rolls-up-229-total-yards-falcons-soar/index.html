Robinson rolls up 229 total yards, Falcons soar

Source: Marc Raimondi • 12/30/2025

Veteran running back Bijan Robinson had a career-high 195 yards rushing on 22 carries, and another 34 yards receiving on five receptions Monday night as the Atlanta Falcons authored a 27-24 victory over the Los Angeles Rams at Mercedes-Benz Stadium.
