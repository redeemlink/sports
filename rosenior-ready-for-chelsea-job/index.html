Rosenior 'ready' for Chelsea job

Source: BBC Sport • 1/6/2026

Speaking in his last press conference as Strasbourg manager, Liam Rosenior says taking the job at Chelsea is the right move for his career. The 41-year-old has spent the last 18-months at the Blues' sister club Strasbourg after he was sacked from Hull City in 2024.
