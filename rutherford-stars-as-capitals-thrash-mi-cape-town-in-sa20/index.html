Rutherford stars as Capitals thrash MI Cape Town in SA20

Source: Google News • 12/31/2025

West Indies all-rounder Sherfane Rutherford starred with bat and ball as Pretoria Capitals thrashed MI Cape Town by 85 runs in the SA20. Rutherford smashed an unbeaten 47 off 15 balls at the backend of Capitals' innings to help them post 220-5 after opting to bat first.
