Salah seals Egypt win over Benin to reach Africa Cup quarterfinals

Source: CIARÁN FAHEY • 1/5/2026

Mohamed Salah scored with the last kick in extra time to seal's Egypt's 3-1 win over Benin in the Africa Cup of Nations last 16 on Monday. The Pharaohs dug deep after being surprisingly held to 1-1 in normal time after Benin's Jodel Dossou pounced on a rebound to level the game in the 83rd minute.
