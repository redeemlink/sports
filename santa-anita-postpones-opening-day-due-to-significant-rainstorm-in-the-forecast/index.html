Santa Anita postpones opening day due to significant rainstorm in the forecast

Source: Google News • 12/21/2025

Santa Anita is postponing the opening day of its 89th winter racing season by two days until Dec. 28 because of an expected significant rainstorm. The meeting usually opens the day after Christmas. The delay should allow turf races to remain on the turf course for Dec. 28.
