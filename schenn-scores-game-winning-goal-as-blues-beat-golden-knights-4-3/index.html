Schenn scores game-winning goal as Blues beat Golden Knights 4-3

Source: WARREN MAYES • 1/2/2026

Brayden Schenn scored the game-winning goal at 18:27 of the third period, leading the St. Louis Blues to a 4-3 victory over the Vegas Golden Knights on Friday. Oskar Sundquist, Justin Faulk and Alexey Toropchenko also scored for the Blues. Joe Hofer recorded 22 saves.
