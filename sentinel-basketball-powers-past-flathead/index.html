Sentinel basketball powers past Flathead

Source: Google News • 1/14/2026

Jan. 14—Avery Ogren scored 19 points as Missoula Sentinel powered past Flathead with a strong fourth quarter to take a 51-40 win at Flathead High School Tuesday in Western AA girls basketball. Lucy Pfhaler had 11 for the Spartans (4-3 overall, 1-2 in Western AA games), while Andrin Reimers added 10.
