Sinner has quick win in start of Australian Open three-peat bid

Source: Google News • 1/20/2026

Jannik Sinner started his bid for a third straight Australian Open with a quicker than expected first-round victory over Hugo Gaston on Tuesday. Sinner led 6-2, 6-1 after 1 hour 8 minutes when the 93rd ranked Gaston retired with an undisclosed injury.
