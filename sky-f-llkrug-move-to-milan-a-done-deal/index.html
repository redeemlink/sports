Sky: Füllkrug move to Milan a done deal

Source: Google News • 12/22/2025

Germany forward Niclas Füllkrug is set to join AC Milan on a six-month loan from West Ham United, according to Sky. The broadcaster reported on Monday that the seven-time European champions had reached agreement on the January deal, which includes an option to buy the former Bundesliga striker.
