Source: Jags extend Van Lanen for 3 years, $51M

Source: Michael Dirocco • 1/3/2026

The Jaguars and offensive lineman Cole Van Lanen agreed on a three-year, $51 million extension, a source told ESPN's Adam Schefter. The deal includes $32.5 million guaranteed and could be worth up to $55.5 million with incentives.
