Sources: Oregon TE Sadiq to declare for NFL draft

Source: Pete Thamel • 1/14/2026

Oregon tight end Kenyon Sadiq, Mel Kiper's No. 1-ranked player at the position, will leave the program early to declare for the NFL draft, sources told ESPN's Pete Thamel on Tuesday, after a season in which he caught 51 passes for 560 yards for the Ducks.
