Sources: TCU's Hoover picks IU after campus visit

Source: Dave Wilson • 1/4/2026

For the third time in three seasons, Indiana has added to its quarterback room via the transfer portal, as TCU's Josh Hoover committed to the Hoosiers, sources told ESPN's Pete Thamel on Sunday. He will enroll at the Big Ten school this month.
