Stuttgart midfielder Stiller doubtful for Europa League clash at Roma

Source: Google News • 1/21/2026

VfB Stuttgart key player Angelo Stiller is doubtful for the Europa League match at Roma on Thursday. Coach Sebastian Hoeness said in a news conference on Wednesday that the likelihood of the midfielder playing is "rather low" and that he didn't train with the team a day before the match.
