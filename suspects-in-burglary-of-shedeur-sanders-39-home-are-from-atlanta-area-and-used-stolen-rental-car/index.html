Suspects in burglary of Shedeur Sanders&#39; home are from Atlanta area and used stolen rental car

Source: JOE REEDY • 1/8/2026

The four suspects involved in the burglary of Shedeur Sanders' home on Nov. 16 are from Atlanta and used a stolen rental car in the break-in, authorities said Thursday. The Medina County Sheriff's Office also said during a briefing that the group had robbed others, including professional athletes.
