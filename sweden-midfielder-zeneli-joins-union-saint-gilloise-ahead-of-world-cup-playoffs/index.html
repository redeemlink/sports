Sweden midfielder Zeneli joins Union Saint-Gilloise ahead of World Cup playoffs

Source: Google News • 1/13/2026

Sweden midfielder Besfort Zeneli signed for Union Saint-Gilloise on Tuesday, and will be eligible for the Champions League knockout phase if the Belgian league leader advances. Union said the 23-year-old Zeneli signed a 4½ -year deal and did not state the transfer fee paid to Swedish club Elfsborg.
