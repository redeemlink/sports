Swiss skier Rast leads World Cup GS with Americans Moltzan, Shiffrin looming in 2nd and 4th

Source: Google News • 1/3/2026

Paula Moltzan and Mikaela Shiffrin finished within striking distance of Swiss leader Camille Rast in the opening run of a women’s World Cup giant slalom on Saturday. Shiffrin holds the women’s record of 22 World Cup wins in the discipline.
