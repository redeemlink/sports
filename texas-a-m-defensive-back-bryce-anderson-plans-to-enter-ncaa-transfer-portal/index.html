Texas A&M defensive back Bryce Anderson plans to enter NCAA Transfer Portal

Source: Google News • 12/30/2025

Texas A&M defensive back Bryce Anderson is planning to enter the NCAA Transfer Portal, On3’s Pete Nakos reports. He’s spent the past four seasons in College Station. Over that time, Anderson has accumulated 117 total tackles, six pass deflections, 1.5 sacks, one interception and two forced fumbles.
