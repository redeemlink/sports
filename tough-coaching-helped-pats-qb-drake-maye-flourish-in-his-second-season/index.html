Tough coaching helped Pats QB Drake Maye flourish in his second season

Source: ROB MAADDI • 1/27/2026

Drake Maye got the tough coaching he wanted and thrived. The 23-year-old Maye is one victory away from being the youngest quarterback in NFL history to win a Super Bowl. The Patriots went from 4-13 under Jerod Mayo in Maye’s rookie season to 14-3.
