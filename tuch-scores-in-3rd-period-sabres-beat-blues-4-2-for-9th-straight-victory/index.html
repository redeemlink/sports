Tuch scores in 3rd period, Sabres beat Blues 4-2 for 9th straight victory

Source: DAVID SOLOMON • 12/30/2025

Zach Benson and Alex Tuch scored and the Buffalo Sabres beat the St. Louis Blues 4-2 on Monday night for their ninth straight victory. Noah Ostlund also scored, Peyton Krebs had an empty-netter and Alex Lyon made 16 saves for Buffalo.
