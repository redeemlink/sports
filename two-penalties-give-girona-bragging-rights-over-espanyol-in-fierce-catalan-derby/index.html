Two penalties give Girona bragging rights over Espanyol in fierce Catalan derby

Source: Google News • 1/16/2026

Vladyslav Vanat scored penalties in stoppage time of each half as Girona beat Espanyol 2-0 in a fiercely contested Catalan derby in La Liga on Friday. It was the first time Girona has won three on the trot since November 2024. Espanyol remained fifth.
