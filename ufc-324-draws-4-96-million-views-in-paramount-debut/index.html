UFC 324 Draws 4.96 Million Views in Paramount+ Debut

Source: Google News • 1/26/2026

The UFC kicked off their Paramount+ partnership with a good viewership turnout for Saturday night’s event. UFC 324 pulled a live average minute audience of 4.96 million views during the main card, with a 5.93 peak concurrent streams. That makes it the largest ever exclusive live event on Paramount+
