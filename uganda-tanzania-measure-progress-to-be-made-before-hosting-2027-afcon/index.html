Uganda, Tanzania measure progress to be made before hosting 2027 AFCON

Source: Google News • 12/29/2025

Uganda and Tanzania will co-host the 2027 Africa Cup of Nations with neighbours Kenya and the countries are getting a sense of how much progress they must still make on the pitch at the ongoing edition in Morocco.Tanzania are appearing at just their fourth Cup of Nations and have never won a game.
