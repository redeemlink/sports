USC transfer LB Matai Tagoa’i signs with Arizona

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

USC transfer linebacker Matai Tagoa’i has signed to play with USC in the 2026 season, On3’s Pete Nakos has learned. He played one season with the Trojans and will have three years of eligibility remaining. Tagoa’i appeared in eight games as a true freshman this past season and recorded four tackles.
