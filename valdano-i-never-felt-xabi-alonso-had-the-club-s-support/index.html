Valdano: “I Never Felt Xabi Alonso Had the Club’s Support”

Source: Google News • 1/12/2026

Speaking on El Tercer Tiempo on Movistar+, Jorge Valdano analyzed Xabi Alonso’s departure from Real Madrid and described a tenure marked by instability and a lack of institutional backing. “I saw Xabi Alonso as unstable from the first day. I never felt he had the support of the club,” Valdano said.
