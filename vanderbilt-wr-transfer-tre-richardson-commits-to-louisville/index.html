Vanderbilt WR transfer Tre Richardson commits to Louisville

Source: Google News • 1/9/2026

Former Vanderbilt WR Tre Richardson has committed to Louisville out of the NCAA transfer portal, On3 has learned. Richardson spent one season with the Commodores. Richardson arrived at Vanderbilt after spending one year at Washburn (Kansas) in 2024. He had a breakout year for the Commodores, though.
