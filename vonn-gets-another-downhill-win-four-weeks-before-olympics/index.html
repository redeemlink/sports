Vonn gets another downhill win four weeks before Olympics

Source: Google News • 1/10/2026

American great Lindsey Vonn proved her class at age 41 again when she stormed to a second downhill season victory in Austria on Saturday, less than a month before the Olympics in Italy. Vonn was unstoppable on a shortened course in Altenmarkt-Zauchensee as she claimed an 84th career victory.
