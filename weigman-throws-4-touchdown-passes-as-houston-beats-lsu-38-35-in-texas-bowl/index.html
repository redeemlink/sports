Weigman throws 4 touchdown passes as Houston beats LSU 38-35 in Texas Bowl

Source: Google News • 12/28/2025

Conner Weigman threw for 236 yards and four touchdowns as Houston rallied from an early deficit for a 38-35 victory over LSU in the Texas Bowl on Saturday night. Weigman finished 27-of-36 passing and rushed for 56 yards.
