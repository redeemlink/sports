Who Jack Nicklaus named as the greatest golfer of all time, even ahead of Tiger Woods

Jack Nicklaus has revealed who he believes is the greatest golfer of all time, and it’s not Tiger Woods. The 18-time major champion named Bobby Jones as his ultimate benchmark, describing the legendary amateur as the sport’s most complete and timeless player.
