Winter, Rapp combine for 36 points as Wisconsin beats Central Michigan 88-61

Source: Google News • 12/23/2025

Nolan Winter and Austin Rapp combined to score 36 points as Wisconsin beat Central Michigan 88-61 on Monday. Winter had 18 points on 6-of-9 shooting to go with eight rebounds. Rapp also had 18 points on 7-of-10 shooting and 4 of 7 from behind the arc to go with seven rebounds off the bench.
