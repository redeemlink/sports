Wright scores tiebreaking goal in 3rd period as Kraken rally from 2 goals down and beat Rangers 4-2

Source: Google News • 1/13/2026

Shane Wright scored the tiebreaking goal in the third period, and the Seattle Kraken rallied from two goals down and beat the New York Rangers 4-2 on Monday night. Eeli Tolvanen, Jordan Eberle and Jared McCann also scored for the Kraken, and Philipp Grubauer had 19 saves.
